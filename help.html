You can add this we page as a bookmark on your phone, so it will look and basically act like an app - iPhone and Android instructions.

When you sign up, you'll need to do so using a Google account. If you have issues, contact Ryan Millan and Michael Stanfa and we'll go from there

When you return to the app, please be sure to use the same Google account each time. It should auto-log you in with the correct account. If you have multiple Google accounts logged in on your device, it may log you in with the wrong account. You'll notice this if you don't see a record or see that you haven't made any picks when you expect to see some. Just sign-out and sign back in. Contact stanfa.michael@gmail.com if this happens and we'll get everything cleaned up.

At this time, you will not be receiving notifications from this app. Lines will be set each week and reflected here. If every game has +-1.5 as the line, don't pick. Lines default to +-1.5 until Millan sets them.

When making your picks, be patient. This is not a perfect web app. You may need to reload the page.

Picks lock 30 minutes before game time. All times on this app are listed as EASTERN. Pick accordingly.

At this time, scores are updated manually by a few people in this group. Your scores and record will be reflected as the game days complete.

Nobody will be able to see your picks except for you.

If you have suggestions or ideas for improvement, please contact stanfa.michael@gmail.com

This was built to help out Millan. He puts in a LOT of time into this pick'em. Using this app will alleviate his time and give you access to lines and standings at your fingertips.
